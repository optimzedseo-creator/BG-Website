Rates & engagement models

Structured around what you need.

No two engagements are the same, so I don’t publish one-size-fits-all prices. These are the four ways we typically work together — pick the shape that fits, reach out, and you’ll get a customized quote scoped to your situation.

Tier 1 · Monthly engagement

Fractional CMO

Executive marketing leadership, embedded in your business on a monthly cadence. I take ownership of the growth agenda — the strategy, the systems, and the team — without the cost of a full-time hire.

  • A defined monthly commitment, scoped to your stage
  • Owns strategy, demand, attribution, and operating rhythm
  • Built to hand you the keys, not create dependence

Consulting retainer

A standing seat at your table. Strategy, AI, and marketing-operations counsel on retainer — so when the expensive decisions come up, you already have someone who knows your business in the room.

  • Ongoing access, structured around a monthly retainer

Hourly sessions

Focused working time on a single problem — an audit, a teardown, a plan you want pressure-tested, or a decision that needs a second set of experienced eyes before you commit.

  • Billed by the hour, no long-term commitment

Custom & hybrid

Your situation doesn’t fit a neat box — a blend of the tiers above, a defined project, or something with very specific requirements. We’ll design a structure that matches the work, then price it to fit.
