Frequently asked questions

Asked before. Answered straight.

The questions that come up before nearly every engagement — answered the way I’d answer them on a call. If yours isn’t here, ask it directly.

When can you start?+

Once we finalize the details and the scope of the job. Exact timing also depends on the current queue — engagements are taken on in the order they’re scoped, so the earlier you reach out, the sooner the start date.

What are your prices?+

Every job is unique, so I can’t give an estimate without knowing what your needs are. What I can tell you is how engagements are structured — fractional CMO, retainer, hourly, or custom — and once you share the shape of the work, you’ll get a real, customized quote.

Is this in person, hybrid, or remote?+

Mainly remote. It keeps engagements efficient and puts your budget into the work instead of travel. If there’s a moment where being in the room genuinely matters, raise it during scoping and we’ll talk it through.

Does your fee include overhead, like ad spend or tools?+

No — the fee covers the consultancy itself: the strategy, the execution, and the counsel. Media budgets, software licenses, and other program costs are separate, and they stay in your name — you keep ownership of the accounts and the data.

What kinds of companies do you work with?+

B2C brands, SaaS platforms, and multi-location operators — a client roster spanning 10+ industries, from founder-led companies to Fortune 500 names. If you’re not sure you fit, the fastest way to find out is to send the brief.

How does an engagement begin?+

Two doors, same room: send a short brief through the contact page, or schedule a 30-minute intro call. Either way, we agree on scope, structure, and a customized quote before any work begins.

Do you take speaking engagements?+

Yes — keynotes, panels, and working sessions on market strategy, competition, and AI. Topics and past stages are on the speaking page.

No form-letter replies — send your question and it lands with me, not a team inbox.
